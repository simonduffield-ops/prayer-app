Daily Prayer Tools

"Be still and know that I am God" - Psalm 46:10

The Examen

A reflective prayer practice to review your day with God, finding His presence in the ordinary moments.

Lectio Divina

Sacred reading that allows God's Word to transform your heart through meditative engagement with Scripture.

Adoration

Worshipful meditation on Scripture that draws your heart into deeper love and reverence for God.

The Examen

"And whatever you do, whether in word or deed, do it all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks to God the Father through him" - Colossians 3:17

1
Presence - Become Aware of God

Take a deep breath and acknowledge that God is with you right now. Rest in His presence and invite the Holy Spirit to guide your reflection.

Pause here and simply be with God...

2
Gratitude - Replay the Day with Thanksgiving

Think through your day like watching a movie. What moments brought you joy? What gifts did God provide? Thank Him for both the obvious blessings and the small graces you might have missed.

Write or reflect on 3-5 things you're grateful for from today...

3
Grace - Recognize God's Movement

Where did you sense God's presence or guidance today? In what moments did you feel His love, peace, or strength? How did He provide or protect?

Notice how God was active in your day...

4
Forgiveness - Acknowledge What Needs Healing

What moments from today need God's forgiveness and healing? Where did you fall short of love? Bring these honestly to Jesus and receive His grace.

Confess and receive forgiveness...

5
Grace for Tomorrow - Look Ahead with Hope

What do you anticipate in tomorrow? Ask for God's grace, wisdom, and presence for the day ahead. Surrender your plans to Him.

Pray for tomorrow's grace...

"Father, thank You for walking with me today. Help me to rest in Your love tonight and to trust You with tomorrow. Amen."

✓ Examen completed for today!

Lectio Divina

"Blessed is the one... whose delight is in the law of the Lord, and who meditates on his law day and night" - Psalm 1:1-2

"Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest. Take my yoke upon you and learn from me, for I am gentle and lowly in heart, and you will find rest for your souls." - Matthew 11:28-29
1
Lectio - Read

Read the passage above slowly and attentively. What word or phrase stands out to you? Read it again, even more slowly.

Write the word or phrase that catches your attention...

2
Meditatio - Meditate

Repeat your word or phrase quietly. What is God saying to you through these words? How do they connect to your life right now?

Reflect on what God might be speaking to you...

3
Oratio - Pray

Respond to God in prayer. Share your heart - your joys, concerns, needs, and gratitude. Let this passage shape your prayer.

Pray in response to God's word...

4
Contemplatio - Rest

Simply rest in God's presence. Let go of words and thoughts. Allow His love to surround you in silence.

Rest silently in God's presence for 2-3 minutes...

Focus Word: "Rest"
Carry this word with you today as a reminder of God's invitation.

✓ Lectio Divina completed for today!

Adoration

"And we all, who with unveiled faces contemplate the Lord's glory, are being transformed into his image with ever-increasing glory" - 2 Corinthians 3:18

"Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty, who was, and is, and is to come... You are worthy, our Lord and God, to receive glory and honor and power, for you created all things, and by your will they were created and have their being." - Revelation 4:8,11
1
Acknowledge - See God's Holiness

Read the passage slowly. What does this reveal about God's character? How does this truth about who He is affect your heart?

What do you see about God's character in this passage?

2
Adore - Worship His Nature

Speak words of praise and worship based on what you've discovered about God. Use your own words to honor who He is.

Worship God for who He is...

3
Surrender - Respond in Humility

How does seeing God's greatness change your perspective on your concerns? Surrender your will and trust to Him.

Surrender your heart and concerns to Him...

4
Transformation - Be Changed by His Glory

Ask God to transform you by His glory. How might living in awareness of this truth about God change how you live today?

How is God calling you to be transformed?

Today's Focus: God's Holiness
Meditate on His perfect purity and majesty throughout your day.
"Holy God, You alone are worthy of all praise. Transform me by Your glory that I might reflect Your character in all I do. Amen."
